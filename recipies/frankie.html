This is the recipe for frankie

Description:

The veg frankie is a popular Indian street-style wrap filled with spicy, pan-seared potato filling seasoned with chaat masala, herbs, and lemon juice. Rolled inside a buttery paratha or soft roti, it’s layered with tangy chutneys, sliced onions, and optional cheese for added richness. Each bite bursts with bold, zesty flavors—making it a perfect on-the-go snack or light meal.

Ingredients

  • Boiled potatoes
  • Onion, green chilli, coriander
  • Chat masala, garam masala, salt
  • Roti or paratha
  • Green chutney or ketchup
  • Onion slices
  • Cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Mash boiled potatoes and mix with chopped onions, green chilies, coriander, and spices.
  2. Form into rolls and shallow-fry or sauté until golden.
  3. Warm or lightly toast roti or paratha with butter or ghee.
  4. Spread green chutney or ketchup on the wrap.
  5. Add the filling, top with onion slices and optional cheese, roll it up and serve.
