This is the recipe for pizza

Description:

Veg pizza is a cheesy, oven-baked delight with a crispy golden crust, spread with rich tomato sauce and generously topped with fresh veggies like bell peppers, mushrooms, olives, onions, and sweet corn. Melted mozzarella blankets the toppings, while herbs like oregano and chili flakes add aroma and spice. It’s a comforting, customizable dish that’s perfect for sharing—or savoring solo.

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Vegetables (capsicum, onion, tomatoes, corn)
  • oregano, chilli flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (430°F).
  2. Spread pizza sauce over the base evenly.
  3. Add cheese and top with your choice of vegetables.
  4. Sprinkle oregano and chili flakes.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes until cheese melts and crust is golden. Slice and serve.
